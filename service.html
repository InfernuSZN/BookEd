FREQUENTLY ASK QUESTIONS

What products do you sell?

We offer a wide range of books, from textbooks to fiction, as well as a variety of educational supplies like stationery, art materials, and classroom resources. Whether you're a student, teacher, parent, or lifelong learner, we have something for everyone!



How do I place an order?

Simply browse through our store, add the items you need to your cart, and proceed to checkout. We offer easy payment options, including credit cards, debit cards, and other secure payment methods. Once your order is placed, we'll handle the rest!



How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary depending on your location. Most orders are processed within 1-2 business days, and standard shipping takes anywhere from 3-7 business days. You’ll receive a tracking number once your order ships so you can follow along the way!



Can I cancel or change my order after placing it?

We aim to process orders quickly, so we can only cancel or modify an order if it hasn’t been shipped yet. If you need to make a change, please contact our support team as soon as possible, and we’ll do our best to help.



What is your refund policy?

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase! If for any reason you are not happy with your order, you can request a refund within 30 days of receiving your items. To initiate a refund, please contact our customer support team, and we’ll guide you through the process.



If you have any more questions regarding our store, please feel free to contact us on our email.
